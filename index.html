Privacy Policy for SmartPad

Effective date: February 20, 2026

Thank you for using SmartPad. This Privacy Policy explains how SmartPad handles your information. SmartPad is designed as an offline-first notes application that prioritizes your privacy and data security. We are committed to ensuring that your personal data remains under your control.

Overview

SmartPad does not require account registration, login, or personal identification. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers operated by SmartPad.

Information Stored on Your Device

The following types of information may be stored locally on your device to enable app functionality:

  • Notes content, titles, checklist items, and folder organization
  • Timestamps such as creation and modification dates
  • App preferences such as theme, font size, sorting options, and layout settings
  • Backup or restore content that you manually copy or paste

This data remains stored only on your device and is not transmitted to SmartPad servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally is used solely to provide the app’s core functionality, including:

  • Creating, editing, deleting, and organizing notes
  • Providing search, pinning, and folder management features
  • Saving your preferences for a consistent user experience
  • Restoring your data when you use the backup and restore feature

Data Collection

SmartPad does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or device identifiers.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

SmartPad does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

  • Your notes are never uploaded to SmartPad servers
  • Your data is not shared with advertisers or analytics providers
  • Data is shared only when you explicitly use system features such as sharing content to another app

Data Storage and Retention

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it
  • You can delete notes and folders at any time within the app
  • You can remove all data by clearing the app’s storage in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored app data

Security

SmartPad relies on your device’s built-in security protections, including application sandboxing and operating system security controls. We recommend using your device’s screen lock, PIN, or biometric authentication to further protect your data.

If you use clipboard backup features, please note that clipboard data may be temporarily accessible to other apps depending on your device and system version.

Internet Access

SmartPad does not require internet access for its core functionality. Any optional internet-related features will clearly inform users before use.

Children’s Privacy

SmartPad does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since SmartPad does not collect any personal data, it is safe for general audiences.

Third-Party Services

SmartPad does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking services that collect personal user data.

Your Rights and Control

  • You have full control over your data
  • You can edit, export, or delete your notes anytime
  • You can uninstall the app to permanently remove all stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements, legal requirements, or new features. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date at the top of this page.
