SmartPad Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 20, 2026

SmartPad is an offline-first notes app. Your notes, folders, checklist items, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. SmartPad does not require account registration and does not upload your note content to a SmartPad cloud service.

Data we process on-device

  • Notes content, titles, checklist data, timestamps, and folder links
  • App preferences such as theme, default font size, and default sorting
  • Optional backup/restore text that you choose to copy to clipboard

How your data is used

  • To create, edit, sort, and organize notes in the app
  • To provide features such as pinning, search, and folder management
  • To restore data if you paste a backup created by you

Data sharing

  • SmartPad does not sell your personal data
  • SmartPad does not share your note content with ad networks
  • System Share sends data only to apps you choose

Retention and deletion

  • Data stays on your device until deleted by you
  • You can delete notes/folders anytime
  • You can clear app data from Android settings

Security

  • SmartPad uses device app sandbox/local storage protections
  • Use screen lock/biometrics for stronger protection
  • Clipboard backups may be visible to other apps while in clipboard

Children's privacy

SmartPad is not designed to collect personal data from children.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated when app features or compliance requirements change.
